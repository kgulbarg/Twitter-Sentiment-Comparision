About this tool:

Twitter has emerged as a major micro-blogging website, having over 100 million users generating over 500 million tweets every day. With such large audience, Twitter has consistently attracted users to convey their opinions and perspective about any issue, brand, company or any other topic of interest. Due to this reason, Twitter is used as an informative source by many organizations, institutions and companies.

This tool helps you to understand the public opinion towards an electronic product and helps you in decided which product is the best among your chioces. It uses the data from the forms to query through Twitter for sentiment of the product. You can also use this tool to understand the opinion towards brands of a product.

Compare products of different models of a brand to know which is the most accepted.

Compare products of different brands and know which has the best brand value for the product.
